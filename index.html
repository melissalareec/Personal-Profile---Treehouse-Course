About Me

Hey! I'm an educator and librarian with project background in web design, user experiences, photography, and curation. My work in public education for a (near) decade has helped me build up a wealth of experience in project management, user research and analytics, data-driven strategic planning, UX, and one-on-one personal communication.

Outside of formal work, I've worked as a freelance photographer, curated and published photobooks, and consulted on web design and social media strategy for small businesses and personal portfolios. Sometimes it feels like this mix of experiences and skills is random, but the unifying thread is always based in bridging content and user whther in education, arts, or business. I'd like to see my technical skills in front-end development open up new doors professionally and in my creative projects.
